# ``SwiftBIP39``

Generate, validate, and derive BIP-39 recovery phrases in Swift.

## Overview

SwiftBIP39 provides:

- BIP-39 English mnemonic generation
- phrase parsing and checksum validation
- seed derivation with optional passphrase
